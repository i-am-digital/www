Template Installation

  1. FTP Upload:
    • Open up your FTP manager and connect to your hosting
    • Browse to required directory (Normally public_html)
    • Upload the files inside mirros folder.

Customize

How to Change Header Logo

Find class="navbar-brand" and replace inside img src="img/icons/top-icon-black.png" with your logo location

How to Change Footer Logo

Find class="footer-logo" and replace inside img src="img/footer/footer-logo.png" with your logo location

How to Change Footer text

Find class="copyright" and replace inside <a> </a> with your copyright text

How to Change Contact form Email Addresss

Go to line number 315 on mail-process.php and replace email address with your email address.
